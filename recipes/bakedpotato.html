Baked Potatoes

Fig. 1: Baked Potatoes are a good side choice or snack.

Who has never proved those hot delicious baked potatoes? Whith ketchup or mayonnaisem that's a classic for children and adults. Wanna learn how to do it? Don't worry, We'll going to guide you through this AMAZING recipe:

Ingredients

Serving: 2 people

  • 3x potatoes in 1/4 pieces
  • 1x garlic cloves
  • Salt at will
  • Paprika at will
  • Rosemary at will

Steps

  1. Chop the potatoes into pieces of 1/4 of size each
  2. Grease a platter to go to oven with either butter or olive oil
  3. Put the pieces of potatoes in the platter respecting a space between them
  4. Season them with salt, paprika and rosemary. You may use some oil to get the potatoes stickier
  5. Heat the oven to 180 degress celsius and put the platter inside
  6. At 30 minutes, flip the potatoes and let it bake for another 30 minutes until it's golden
  7. Serve them with mayo, ketchup or another sauce

Everyone will love it!
