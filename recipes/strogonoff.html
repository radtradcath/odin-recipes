Strogonoff

Fig. 1: Strogonoff with rice is a good choice.

The strogonoff is fairly popular in many countries, especially Brazil. It features rice, some protein of your choice and goes very well with rice and chips or shoestring potatoes. We're going to guide you through this AMAZING recipe:

Ingredients

Serving: 4 people

  • 2x onion
  • 3x garlic cloves
  • 1x can of milk cream
  • 2x spoons o ketchup
  • 1x spoon os mustard sauce
  • 500g of red meat/diced chicken chest
  • 1x spoon of english sauce to add colour
  • 1x cup of rice
  • Potato chips or shoestring as your preference

Steps

  1. Cut onions and garlic cloves into little pieces and fry them with a bit of oil
  2. Split onions and garlics in half and pour them into two separated pans for the protein and rice
  3. Add the rice into one of the pans and cover it with 2x cups of water. Boil, cover and set the fire to minimum until all water evaporates
  4. In the other pan, add your protein and fry it until it's golden.
  5. Add the ketchup, mustard sauce and english sauce and mix them. Note: you can separate this mix and save it in the fridge to continue later. You must do the next step only when ready to serve.
  6. Pour the milk cream into the mix and warm the mixture in fire

Thats it! Serve it in a plate with rice and potatoes. Everyone will love it!
